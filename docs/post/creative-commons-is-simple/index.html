Creative Commons is simple

July 8, 2021
licensing

We all shy away from licensing.

You have read some license. They are usually terse and hard to read. I guess they can only be read by lawyers.

Licensing is something that I find hard to . A few days back, I understood about CC license and so I thought I’ll share it here in this blog.

Without going deep into copyrights and intellectual property rights, If I create something, be it a photo, a video, or a book, I have a copyright on it and anyone who wants to use, modify or sell it needs my permission.

The other extreme is the public domain works which can be used, modified or sold by anyone without the need to ask permission.

I think the reason for the widespread use of CC licenses is that it is simple to understand. And we the licensor? have the flexibility to choose between the 7 licenses

  • Can be used on photos, videos, art, music, databases, slides, books
  • Are irrevocable
    • We can stop sharing But anyone who has a copy will be permitted to use it with the old license
  • I used to think that copyright was wrong
  • Mentioning the license helps express our expectations in an explicit manner, about how people can use that.
  • Between Copyright and Public domain (CC0) there is a continuum, filled by 7 CC licenses.

4 rights

BY (Attribution)

  • You can do whatever with it as long as you attribute the work to me and give a link back to the site

SA (Share Alike)

  • You cannot change the license (or maybe make less restrictive)

NC (Non Commercial)

  • You can only use it for non commercial purposes.

ND (Non Derviative)

  • The content should not be modified

Conditions

No liability
